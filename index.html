Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new subscription?
Tap the "+" button in the top right corner of the main screen. Fill in the subscription information: name, cost, billing day, period (month/year), and category. Tap "Save".
How do I change the currency?
Go to Settings (gear icon) and select your desired currency from the list. All amounts will be automatically recalculated.
How do I restore a deleted subscription?
Deleted subscriptions are stored in the "Recently Deleted" section in Settings for 30 days. You can restore a subscription or delete it permanently.
How do reminders work?
The app reminds you about upcoming charges a specified number of days before the payment date. You can configure the number of reminder days when creating or editing a subscription.
Where is my data stored?
All data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or share your personal information with third parties. Your data remains private.
Is the app available on other platforms?
Yes, Subkeep is available on both iOS and macOS. Your data syncs via iCloud (if sync is enabled).
